Monex Lubricants at MotorTec Madrid 2025 | Automotive Trade Show | Engine Oils | Hall 4E10

We’re proud to announce that Monex Lubricants will be exhibiting at MotorTec Madrid 2025, one of Europe’s most influential trade shows in the automotive aftermarket and engine oil industry.

As part of the Özerşah Group, we’ll be live at IFEMA Madrid from April 23 to 26, showcasing our high-performance lubricants engineered for maximum protection and power. This is your chance to meet the brand behind a growing network of distributors and explore the future of engine oils.

  • Event Dates: April 23–26, 2025
  • Hall: 4
  • Stand: 4E10
  • Location: IFEMA Madrid – MotorTec 2025

What is MotorTec Madrid?

MotorTec Madrid is a premier international trade fair focused on automotive services, spare parts, repair technologies, and workshop solutions. Hosted at IFEMA in the heart of Spain, this event brings together industry leaders, technical experts, manufacturers, and automotive brands from across the globe.

This year, Monex is proud to be part of this prestigious platform, offering our latest range of lubricants designed to meet the demands of modern engines and global standards.

Why Visit the Monex Lubricants Stand?

If you’ll be attending MotorTec Madrid, be sure to visit us in Hall 4 – Stand 4E10. Here’s what you can expect:

  • Explore our complete portfolio of high-performance engine oils for cars, trucks, and industrial vehicles.
  • Meet our expert team and discuss distribution opportunities across Europe and beyond.
  • Discover how Monex combines technology, protection, and performance to support modern engines.

Whether you’re in the aftermarket, maintenance sector, or looking to partner with a reliable lubricant brand – this is your chance to connect with Monex face-to-face.
